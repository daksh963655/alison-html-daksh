About Aliso Dental Care

Founded in 1999 by Dr. Silvia Vallis, Aliso Dental Care has been serving the Orange County community with comprehensive dental solutions.

    We utilize the latest dental technology to ensure effective treatments and exceptional results for our patients.

  • Patient Comfort

    Our dedicated team prioritizes your comfort, making every visit a pleasant and tailored experience.

Our Vision

To be the leading dental care provider in Orange County, creating beautiful smiles and fostering lasting relationships.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional dental services with a focus on patient comfort and personalized care for optimal oral health.
