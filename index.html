Who we are

About Aliso Dental Care

Founded in 1999 by Dr. Silvia Vallis, Aliso Dental Care has been serving the Orange County community with comprehensive dental solutions.

At Aliso Dental Care, our mission is to deliver exceptional dental care while creating a positive, welcoming environment where every patient feels safe, respected, and truly valued.

Our Dental Services

Learn more about the dental services we provide to help you smile with confidence at every stage of life.

General Dentistry

Comprehensive exams, cleanings, and preventive care to keep your teeth and gums healthy year-round.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Whitening, veneers, and smile design solutions to enhance the appearance and confidence of your smile.

Periodontal Therapy

Targeted care to prevent, manage, and treat gum disease — protecting both your oral and overall health.

Restorative Dentistry

Crowns, bridges, and fillings to repair damaged teeth and restore full function and comfort.

Dentures

Custom full and partial dentures designed to restore your smile, improve speech, and support chewing comfort.

Emergency Dentistry

Same-day appointments available for urgent dental issues like toothaches, chips, or lost fillings.
